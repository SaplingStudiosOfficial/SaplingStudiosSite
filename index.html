About Us

John Gonzalez has always dreamed of becoming a video game developer since he was 10 years old. As a child, he loved to pretend he was a hero and play with sticks outside. His passion for video games and storytelling led him to pursue a career in game development.

Today, John is the founder of Sapling Studios, where he works with a wide variety of creative, passionate people. John's goal is to create games that draw people into a new world, where they can be the heroes of their own stories. He believes that video games have the power to transport players to new places and inspire them to be their best selves not only on the grid, but in the real world.
